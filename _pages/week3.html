    • Tuesday, May 28th - Language (#9/28 of class):

    Learning Objectives:
    1. LO1: Continue to build a classroom culture & discuss science communication
    2. LO2: Apply principles of good science communication
    3. LO3: Describe the basic foundamental principles of attention
    4. LO4: Summarize and critically analyze academic journal articles

    Agenda:

    1. Generate rubric for tweet summary assignment as a class (LO1).
    2. Apply list of science communication principles we generated to handouts with good and bad headlines and lead sentences & clarify headline/lead assignment (LO2).
    3. Demos on visual search, change blindness, selective attentionn (LO3).
    4. Basic principles of selective, shared, and divided attention, including early theories on attention (LO3).
    5. Discuss whether the podcast followed our good principles of science communication & what it did differently from news articles (LO2, LO3).
    6. Summarize and analyze the journal articles on attention (LO4).
    7. Reflections on class and any concerns/questions you, students, have.
